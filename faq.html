Frequently Asked Questions

Click the questions below to reveal the answers. If you still can't find the answer you're looking for, send us an email at webdev@seattleu.edu or call us at (206) 220-8280.

The suggested timeline is to take two courses for four quarters, which is one year of part-time studies.

We are currently going through the process to qualify for US Federal Financial Aid. Until we receive approval, our students cannot receive Financial Aid. However, we are optimistic we will be able to qualify in the near future. If you are reliant on Financial Aid, we suggest that you make sure you have submitted a completed FAFSA form and have contacted our office at (206) 220-8280. We will be able to note your interest in Financial Aid and will process your FAFSA as soon as we are approved.

At this point in time the courses require some in-person meetings. These meetings are considered crucial to the success of our learners because they help to build everyone's professional networks and the allow individuals to become integrated into the Web development community. If you are unable to be in Seattle at all, and still very interested in the program, we encourage you to write us at webdev@seattleu.edu. As we consider additional programs and schedules we value your input.

We do not assume any prior experience with programming, but we require you to take a basic skills test to evaluate your computing ability. The more savvy you are with computers, games, software, and gadgets, then the more likely you are to be successful. You will undoubtedly find yourself challenged, and for some people the process of learning to code can require a significant reworking of how you think about problems. But there is no magic to coding, and anyone who can write and follow a set of directions can learn to code. If you are an avid chef, tailor, carpenter, or crafter, then you are probably creating and following algorithms without realizing it. We provide a supportive environment where everyone can learn the skills they need to build incredible websites, and that does not require you to already know how to write software.
